Do you have lead pipes in your home?

Lead is bad for health, especially for children.

There are lead pipes leading to a lot of homes. The part running up the sidewalk was probably replaced by the city ... but the part that leads from there to the home might not have been.

Lead can flake off in tiny pieces too hard to see and end up in your drinking water – especially if there’s construction going on, or during the warm summer.

What are your pipes are made of? Help us make a map of where lead pipes could be affecting people’s drinking water.

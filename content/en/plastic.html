Your pipes are made of plastic

That’s good. The effect of plastic pipes on health hasn’t been extensively studied, but it’s generally accepted that they’re a good way to go when it comes to plumbing.

There’s a small chance you could still have lead fittings or solder in the plumbing in your house. If you want to check whether there’s lead in your water, request a test from your water utility. If you’re concerned, you could also think about getting a water filter that is certified for lead removal (it should say “NSF/ANSI Standard 53” on the package).

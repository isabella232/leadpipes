Do you have lead pipes in your home?

Lead pipes bring water into a lot of homes. But the metal is connected with behavior and learning problems in kids, and high blood pressure and kidney problems in adults.

We’ll help you find out if your drinking water is at risk. And don’t worry. If you do find lead, we’ll show you what you can do.

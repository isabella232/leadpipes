Your pipes are probably made of copper.

That’s great because they aren’t made of lead – congratulations! But it’s still possible that you might have some lead fittings or solder in the plumbing in your house.

If you want to check if there’s lead in your water, request a test from your water utility. You could also think about getting a water filter that is certified for lead removal (it should say “NSF/ANSI Standard 53” on the package).
